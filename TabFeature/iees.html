Energy Exchange Station

Our proprietary Energy Exchange Stations (EnEx Stations) are engineered around Battery Swapping. Our EnEx Stations don’t just provide a mechanism to swap batteries, but also charge them. These EnEx stations are always connected and are continuously monitoring and optimising our network of batteries.We have created a proprietary communication layer for our systems, so network uptime is not dependent on any external communication medium.In the first phase of our launch, we are building our EnEx Network in major Indian cities, which will will facilitate and service over 1 Lakh 2-wheelers.To offset the energy consumed by our network, we have already sanctioned a 50mW Solar Park. For more information, click here.We will also be installing several Experience Centres which will have solar installations.
