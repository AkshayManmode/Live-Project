Energy Park

One of the major issues facing the world today is energy generation and distribution. This issue will escalate with more markets and users adopting Electric Vehicles. Governments around the world are turning towards renewable sources of energy generation.True to our ethos, we have been working tirelessly on generating clean and sustainable energy from non polluting sources.

Innovation

As an organization, we intend to generate more energy than our ecosystem consumes. We at IORD have a government sanctioned 50mW Solar Park Project underway.This project named IORD's Power Generation & Distribution System situated near Pune, Maharashtra, on a sprawling land of 36 acres. Beyond this, all of our energy exchange experience centres will also have their own solar power installations which will help in reduce power costs owing to net metering.
