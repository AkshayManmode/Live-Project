‘AI’ Based E-Vehicle Battery Introduced, Can Be Turned On And Off With Remote

Pune, 10th August 2022:

How much battery is left for e-two-wheelers and three- wheelers? When will it be depleted? Has the temperature increased? These questions can be answered with ease now. The Pune-based Infinite Orbit Research and Development (IORD) Research Institute in the field of e-vehicles has claimed that these actions are possible now.For the first time, ‘Al’ has been used in a battery.
