About Us

Infinite Orbit Research & Development (IORD) is an organization based out of Pune, India, which has been focused on R&D activities in Transportation System, Electric Mobility, Energy and allied sectors for the past 10+ years. Knowing that our transportation industry is in the process of a once-in-a-lifetime transition, over the past few years, our team has focused our abilities on researching, improving and expediting this transition to Electric Mobility. Through our extensive research, we have developed a sustainable E-Mobility ecosystem that will not just help with this transition, but also help the transportation industry survive and thrive.
