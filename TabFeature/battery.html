Our Battery

Our battery packs are fully Researched, Engineered, Developed and Produced in-house.With a focus on simplifying E-Mobility, we have created a standard battery-pack that fits all use cases - from 2 wheelers, 3 wheelers, to 4 wheeler Cars and even Load carriers.Our Intelligent Battery Management & Control System (BMCS) is based on AI, Machine Learning and Neural Networks which makes the battery self-balancing, self-maintaining and self-correcting.
