WHO WE ARE

Infinite Orbit Research & Development (IORD) is an organization which has been focused on R&D activities in Transportation System, Electric Mobility, Energy and allied sectors for the past 10+ years. Knowing that our transportation industry is in the process of a once in a life-time transition, over the past few years, our team has focused our abilities on researching, improving and expediting this transition to E-Mobility. Through this intense research, we have developed a sustainable E-Mobility ecosystem that will not just help with this transition, but also thrive.
